## Regulatory Landscape

### Simpler Recycling (March 2025)

**Effective**: 31 March 2025

**Requirements for Businesses (10+ employees)**:
- Separate food waste at source
- Separate paper/card
- Separate dry recyclables

**Impact on Commercial Waste Software**:
- Enhanced waste categorization required
- Segregation tracking and reporting
- Compliance monitoring capabilities
- Customer communication tools

**Opportunity**: Councils need systems that can help commercial customers comply with new regulations.

### Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

**Effective**: Phased introduction from 2025

**Key Changes**:
- Producers fund full recycling and disposal costs
- Packaging waste management costs shift to manufacturers
- Reporting requirements increase

**Impact on Software**:
- Packaging categorization and tracking
- Reporting to compliance schemes
- Cost allocation mechanisms

### Landfill Tax Increases

**2025 Rate**: £126/tonne (22% increase from 2024)

**Context**: Average 2.6% CAGR 2015-2024, now sharp increase

**Impact**:
- Stronger incentive for recycling
- Increased focus on waste stream analytics
- ROI case for optimization software

## Pricing Trends

### Common Models

1. **Perpetual License + Maintenance**
   - Upfront license cost
   - Annual maintenance (15-20%)
   - Implementation services separate
   - Declining in popularity

2. **SaaS Subscription**
   - Monthly/annual subscription
   - Per user or per vehicle pricing
   - Implementation included or separate
   - Growing preference

3. **Hybrid Models**
   - Core subscription
   - Add-on modules priced separately
   - Volume-based components

### Price Indicators

**Commercial Waste Software Contracts (UK)**:
- Small councils: £200K-400K (5 years)
