# Competitor Analysis

## Market Overview

The UK waste management software market for local authorities is dominated by a few key players, with Whitespace and Bartec Collective leading in terms of council installations. The market is valued at approximately **USD 600 million (2024)** with projected growth to **USD 1.23 billion by 2032** (9.38% CAGR).

| Customer Base | 4,000+ globally |
| Market Position | Global leader |
| UK Presence | Municipal and commercial waste |

### Products & Features

**AMCS Platform** - Enterprise SaaS:
- End-to-end waste and recycling
- Municipal and commercial waste
- Route optimization
- Bin management
- Weighbridge integration

**AMCS 7 ERP** (current Nottingham system):
- Single operation and invoicing
- Fleet planner integration
- Mobile technology
- On-board weighing

**Key Capabilities**:
- Pay-by-weight (PAYT)
- Scale house and transfer stations
- Contract management with advanced pricing
- Vehicle technology and telematics

### Pricing Features

- Pay per weight
- Weight allowances
- Price breaks and indexing
- Per visit pricing
- Minimum lift charging

### Strengths (as Incumbent)

1. **Existing relationship** - 9 years with council
2. **Data ownership** - All customer and operational data
3. **Operational knowledge** - Understanding of council processes
4. **Low switching cost narrative** - "Why change?"
5. **Global scale** - Large company, ongoing investment

### Weaknesses

1. **Software quality issues** - Reported as "very buggy"
2. **Support responsiveness** - Slow bug fixes described
3. **Customization costs** - Extra fees for reports and changes
4. **Age of current system** - AMCS 7 from 2016 may be dated
5. **Customer service concerns** - Negative reviews on GetApp/Capterra

### Known Issues (from Reviews)

- "Very buggy and poor service"
- Short-term bug fixes, returning issues
- Additional costs for reports and customization
- Accounting features not meeting standards
- Acquisition-related support issues

### Threat Assessment

- **Threat Level**: HIGH (incumbent advantage)
- **Likely Bid**: Yes (defending position)
- **Competitive Approach**: Continuity, upgrade path, switching cost avoidance

## Key Differentiator Opportunities

Based on competitor analysis, opportunities to differentiate:

1. **Oracle Fusion Integration** - No competitor has demonstrated Oracle Fusion integration capability
2. **Support Quality** - AMCS has documented support issues; commit to superior SLAs
3. **Pricing Transparency** - Offer clear, all-inclusive pricing vs hidden customization costs
4. **Modern UX** - Focus on consumer-grade customer portal experience
5. **Commercial Waste Specialization** - Match Bartec's commercial waste depth
